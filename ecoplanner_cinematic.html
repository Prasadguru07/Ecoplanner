03
Problem & Solution

Why does this tool need to exist?

Buildings are responsible for 37% of global CO₂ emissions - more than all cars and planes combined. Yet most architects pick materials based on habit or cost, not carbon data. EcoPlanner closes that gap permanently.

✕   3 problems before EcoPlanner
✕
Guesswork - architects pick materials by habit, not carbon data. No systematic framework exists.
✕
No simulation - energy impact unknown until the building is built and operating.
✕
Cost barrier - professional consultants charge ₹5–20L for this analysis. Out of reach for most projects.
✓   3 things EcoPlanner provides
✓
Physics-based simulation - ISO 13790 model, same standard used in LEED certification worldwide.
✓
Grounded AI - recommendations locked to a real CSV database, zero hallucination risk.
✓
Free & instant - runs in 30 seconds on any laptop. No consultant, no waiting, no cost.
Key insight

A single screenshot showing "Concrete → Hempcrete saved 367 kg CO₂/m² on a 500 m² school" will resonate across LinkedIn, X, and GitHub simultaneously — covering professional, climate, and developer audiences in one post.
