① What is EcoPlanner? — Simple words

Think of it like a smart consultant for building designers. When someone wants to build a house, school, or office, they normally guess which materials to use. EcoPlanner instead calculates exactly how much energy the building will waste — then tells you which eco-friendly materials will save the most CO₂, backed by real data, not opinions.

Real-world analogy: Imagine you're a doctor. Instead of guessing a patient's treatment, you run blood tests first. EcoPlanner runs "blood tests" on a building — measures its energy profile — then prescribes the exact right eco-materials with proof of how much CO₂ each one saves.
